Welcome to PDF Balloon Tool

MechProTools — Quick start guide (shown once)

Open your PDF engineering drawing

Click Open PDF in the sidebar or drag & drop a PDF directly onto the canvas area. Use mouse wheel to zoom, or use the +/− buttons. Press ⊡ to auto-fit the drawing to screen.

Tip: zoom in close to a feature before clicking to place a balloon — more precision with smaller targets.

Three leader types available

Straight / Arrow1 click = 1 balloon placed immediately
Orthogonal 90°3 clicks = 1 balloon with right-angle leader
Select Orthogonal 90° in the Leader dropdown to activate 3-click mode.

Orthogonal 90° — 3 clicks per balloon

Click 1Feature point — where leader tip touches the drawing feature
Click 2Elbow point — where the 90° bend happens in the leader line
Click 3Balloon position — where the circle with number appears

A dashed ghost preview follows your cursor at each step so you can see the path before committing. Press Esc at any time to cancel.

If Click 3 is placed at the same spot as Click 2, no bend is made — it becomes a straight line automatically.

Export your annotated PDF

When done, click Export Annotated PDF. All balloons and leaders are permanently baked into the PDF at full resolution — ready for FAI, inspection reports, or customer delivery.

Use Select/Move mode to drag balloons or leader tips to reposition after placing. Delete individual balloons from the sidebar list.

Tip: you can place balloons across multiple pages — they are tracked per page.
