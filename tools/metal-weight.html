Metal weight calculation formula

Weight = Volume × Density. For a plate: Volume = Length × Width × Thickness. For a round bar: Volume = π/4 × D² × Length. For a tube: Volume = π/4 × (OD² - ID²) × Length. All dimensions should be in consistent units (mm gives mm³, then divide by 1,000,000,000 for m³, multiply by density in kg/m³).

Common material densities

Carbon Steel: 7,850 kg/m³ · Stainless Steel 304: 7,930 · SS 316: 8,000 · Aluminium: 2,700 · Copper: 8,960 · Brass: 8,500 · Titanium: 4,510 · PVC: 1,780 kg/m³
