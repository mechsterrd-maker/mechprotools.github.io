Form tolerances (no datum required)
⏥

Flatness

Surface must lie between two parallel planes separated by the tolerance value. Controls waviness and bowing.

⌭

Straightness

Line element or axis must lie within a tolerance zone. Applied to surface lines or feature axis.

○

Circularity (Roundness)

Each circular cross-section must lie between two concentric circles. Controls ovality and lobing.

⌓

Cylindricity

Entire surface must lie between two coaxial cylinders. Combines circularity, straightness, and taper.

Orientation tolerances (datum required)
⊥

Perpendicularity

Feature must be within tolerance zone perpendicular to datum. Controls 90° relationships.

∠

Angularity

Feature at a specified angle to datum must lie within tolerance zone at that angle.

∥

Parallelism

Feature must lie between two planes/lines parallel to datum within tolerance.

Location tolerances (datum required)
⊕

Position

Feature's actual position must fall within cylindrical/parallel tolerance zone at true position. Most commonly used GD&T symbol.

◎

Concentricity

Median points of all cross-sections must lie within cylindrical tolerance zone coaxial with datum axis.

≡

Symmetry

Median points of opposed features must lie within tolerance zone symmetrical about datum plane.

Runout tolerances (datum required)
↗

Circular Runout

Each circular element must not vary more than tolerance during one full rotation about datum axis.

↗↗

Total Runout

Entire surface must not vary more than tolerance during full rotation. Controls all surface errors simultaneously.

Profile tolerances
⌒

Profile of a Line

Each line element of a surface must lie within 2D tolerance zone. Applied cross-section by cross-section.

⌓

Profile of a Surface

Entire surface must lie within 3D tolerance zone. Most versatile GD&T control — can replace size, form, orientation, location.

Bonus tolerance with MMC

When position tolerance is applied at MMC (Maximum Material Condition), bonus tolerance equals the departure from MMC. If a hole's MMC is ⌀10.0 and actual size is ⌀10.15, bonus = 0.15. Total allowed position tolerance = stated tolerance + bonus.
