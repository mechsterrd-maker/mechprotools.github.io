About MechProTools

MechProTools is a free, browser-based 3D model viewer and engineering toolkit built by a mechanical engineer with 14+ years of plant engineering experience.

Why we built this

Every day, engineers need to quickly view a STEP file from a supplier, check a dimension on a 3D model, or calculate the weight of a pipe run. The existing options are either expensive desktop software or clunky web tools that can't handle real CAD formats.

MechProTools solves this. Open any STEP, IGES, or STL file directly in your browser — on your phone, tablet, or desktop. No installation, no registration, no cost.

Technology

Built with OpenCascade Technology (OCCT) compiled to WebAssembly via occt-import-js, rendered with Three.js. All file processing happens locally in your browser — your CAD files never leave your device.
