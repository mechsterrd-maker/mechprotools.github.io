Online STEP file viewer — how it works

MechProTools is a browser-based 3D CAD viewer that opens STEP (.step, .stp), IGES (.iges, .igs), STL, OBJ, and PLY files directly on any device. It uses OpenCascade Technology (OCCT), the same open-source CAD kernel used in professional software like FreeCAD, to tessellate and render BREP geometry in real-time.

Supported CAD formats

STEP (Standard for the Exchange of Product model data) is the most widely used format for exchanging 3D models between different CAD systems. IGES (Initial Graphics Exchange Specification) is an older format still used in many industries. Both are fully supported by MechProTools's built-in CAD engine.

Who is this for?

Mechanical engineers, plant engineers, piping designers, manufacturing teams, procurement departments, students, and anyone who needs to quickly view a 3D model without installing heavyweight CAD software. Works perfectly for reviewing supplier drawings, checking assembly clearances, or sharing models with clients.

Privacy

Your files never leave your device. All processing happens locally in your browser using WebAssembly. No upload, no server, no tracking of your models.
